About Us

Welcome to YummyFood! We bring the vibrant and spicy flavors of Kathmandu streets directly to you. Our journey started with a passion for Momos and Sekuwa, and now we are sharing that love on wheels. Fresh ingredients, authentic spices, and a whole lot of heart go into every dish we serve.
